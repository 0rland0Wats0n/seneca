Time Commitment and Stipend Payment Policy - Bay Area Programs
In order to establish therapeutic relationships with our youth and families, we require a nine to ten month commitment from all students; beginning in the fall of each year and continuing through to the spring of the following year. Some fieldwork placements can be extended to 12 months (fall to fall) if requested by the student at the time of application. Please note we do not offer winter-start or summer-only fieldwork placements.

MSW interns, MFT trainees, and PsyD practicum students in placement receive $300/mo for working 16 hours per week; $500/mo for students working 24 hours per week; and $700mo for 2nd year students working 24 hours per week who also completed their 1st year internship with Seneca. Please note: Receipts for educational expenses are required for the full amount of your stipend.
